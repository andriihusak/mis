About Us

What`s MIS represents?

Medical Information System (MIS) for complex automation of the main processes of medical institutions of any type: clinics, hospitals with inpatient departments, medical center, private clinic, laboratory.

The modular structure of the system allows you to adjust and adapt it to the specifics of work and specific needs of the medical institution without the need to reorganize existing business processes. There is no need to stop the clinic to implement MIS, and training staff to work with it will be easier and faster.
